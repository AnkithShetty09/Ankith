Information Science & Engineering

The Department of Information Science & Engineering at NMAMIT focuses on the integration of computer science and information systems to solve real-world problems. It offers students training in databases, networking, software engineering, cybersecurity, and emerging fields such as data analytics and cloud computing. With strong industry tie-ups and modern infrastructure, the department nurtures students to become future-ready professionals and innovators in the IT sector.
