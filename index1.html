About Us

Nitte Mahalinga Adyanthaya Memorial Institute of Technology (NMAMIT), Nitte, established in 1986 under the Nitte Education Trust by Justice K. S. Hegde, is a premier engineering institution nestled in a 125-acre eco-friendly campus in the Western Ghats of Karnataka. A constituent college of Nitte (Deemed to be University), it offers B.Tech, M.Tech, and Ph.D. programs across various engineering and applied science disciplines. Accredited with NAAC “A+” and NBA for its UG programs, NMAMIT is recognized for its academic excellence, industry collaborations, and innovation, consistently securing high national rankings. With modern infrastructure, strong research culture, and a commitment to holistic student development, the institute truly lives by its motto, “Strive for Excellence.”
